About

Kelly Munro APSNZ is a New Zealand based nature, wildlife and landscape photographer. She is a member of the Photographic Society of New Zealand (PSNZ), the Porirua Photography Club and the Kapiti Coast Photographic Society. She attained her Licentiate of the Photographic Society of New Zealand (LPSNZ) in 2016, followed by her Associateship of the Photographic Society of New Zealand (APSNZ) in 2019. Her work has featured in exhibitions in both New Zealand and Australia.
